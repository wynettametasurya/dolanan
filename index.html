WHAT IS CONGKLAK?

In short, Congklak is a famous Indonesian traditional game, also known as a part of the Mancala games, which have travelled worldwide. It is played by two players on a board with circular holes with two big holes on the ends and an amount of 84 beads to be moved around the circles.
Congklak in Middle East
Most scholarly believes that it first originated in the Middle East, spread to Africa, and then spread to Asia with Arab traders, and finally came to the Caribbean around 1640 via the African slave trade. However, some other scholars also believe that it’s originated from the Africa.
Congklak in Indonesia
Javanese nobility's young ladies used to play the game of Congklak. It is highly probable that Congklak was introduced to the higher classes by foreign dealers who had close ties to them.

As Congklak gained popularity, more and more regular people started playing it. Congklak is viewed as a "girl's game" in most areas and is only played by young girls and women in their free time. However, it is not restricted to girls and women, Congklak is also played by men and boys in a few areas.

In Sulawesi, Congklak has historically only been played during times of grief, following the loss of a loved one. Playing the game at any other time in Sulawesi can be considered taboo. Meanwhile, in Central Java, during pre-historic times, farmers employed Congklak to foresee the future, determine when to plant and harvest, and calculate the seasons.

LEARN HOW TO PLAY

1. Ensure that you have all the equipment ready — A wooden or plastic board with seven pairs of small circular holes facing each other and two big holes in the left and right sides as each player’ store house & 84 beads to be divide equally to the two players playing.
2. The two players will fill all the 12 small holes with 7 beads each. Once all equipment is set, decide who will start first and the player who start first take out the seeds in one of the holes and filling them in the other small holes of both players in a clockwise manner. While moving, the player can also fill their store house with the seed but not the opponents store house.
3. Where the drop of the last seed takes place determines the next move. If it’s dropped in the current player’s own store house, they can choose to take another seed from their own small holes. If the last seed dropped in a random hole with seeds initially in it, they take all the seeds and again repeat the same procedures again.
4. When the last seed is dropped in a random hole without any seeds, meaning that there’s only one seed in that whole which is the seed that is dropped, then the other player gets their turn.
5. In the end, when all the seeds in the 12 small holes is finished, both players count how many seeds they have in their own store house. The one having the most seeds at the end of the game wins.

BENEFITS

Playing Congklak and other traditional games do have its benefits, among others

Community Engagement

Congklak promotes the development of social and emotional skills. Instead of being at home, spending heaps time with digital devices, it allows children or even teenagers to interact with others.

Brain Training

Congklak is quite a strategic game where it enables training for the child's left brain to think on how to gain more seeds and defeat the opponent. On the other side, it also develops the right brain, training children to cooperate with the opponent. Furthermore, with Congklak being numeracy-focused, it allows children at early stage to learn counting.

Motoric Skills

Congklak requires it players to move seeds from holes to holes, which eventually might develop the players’ motoric skills. It is important to leverage motoric skills every now and then as they makes behavior increasingly functional and flexible.

Focus Training

Utilising Congklak as a form of training, also helps children to focus more on task, allow learning of various contents, and can motivate players to learn more about a topic. Furthermore, it trains the players to be emotionally intelligence as it required patience, honesty, and sportiveness.
